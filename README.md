# doppler-effect-app
This Android application provides a real-time acoustic analysis and visualization of the Doppler shift. It was developed to observe and measure the frequency shift in sound waves generated by moving objects in a real-world environment.

🌟 Features
Real-Time Frequency Analysis: Tracks and registers the pitch of incoming audio continuously.

Live Visualization: Displays the distinct shift in frequency—registering a higher pitch during the approach and a measurable drop upon departure.

Empirical Measurement: Ideal for practical physics, acoustic demonstrations, and educational purposes.

🎥 Demonstration

🚀 How to Use
Stand near a road or path (always prioritize safety and stand at a secure distance from traffic).

Launch the application and grant the necessary microphone permissions.

Wait for a continuous sound source (e.g., a passing car, a siren, or a sustained horn) to approach and pass by your stationary device.

Observe the real-time frequency graph and metrics!
